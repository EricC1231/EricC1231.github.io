About Me

I’m a game developer with hands-on experience in both 2D and 3D gameplay programming, networking (primarily TCP with some UDP), and web development. I’ve worked extensively with Godot and Unity, using C# in both engines and GDScript in Godot for non-performance-critical systems.

Across many of my projects, I’ve taken on a programming lead role—shaping technical direction, implementing core systems, and helping onboard and mentor newer developers. My work spans procedural generation, multiplayer networking, physics-driven gameplay, and rapid prototyping through game jams.
